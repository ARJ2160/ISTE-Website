Our Mission

We help students transform themselves into high performing professionals.

Our mission is to make productive use of technology to accelerate innovation, engage in collaborative learning, and inspire students to reach their greatest potential. At ISTE Students' Chapter, MMCOE, we attach great value to academics as well as creative tendencies. Through ISTE, MMCOE students get various opportunities to enhance technical skills through valuable industry interactions.
