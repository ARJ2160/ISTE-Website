What is ISTE?

Indian Society of Technical Education (ISTE) is a national, professional, non-profit organization driven by the motto of overall development of the technical education system in India.

Some of our Flagship Events

Hacksprint

The most awaited and anticipated flagship event of ISTE SC, MMCOE. It is a national-level hackathon that with problem statements in the domains of AR, Web Development, ML/AI, and App Development.

FACE

Fast Assessment by Company Experts - FACE, is another flagship event of ISTE MMCOE which includes aptitude and psychometric tests, technical evaluation and HR + management interview to help students become industry-ready.

Chimera X (With MANIT)

ISTE MMCOE prominently conducts a zonal round for one of the largest quizzing festivals - CHIMERA-X, the multi-city flagship event of ISTE SC, MANIT, Bhopal. Every year we receive an amazing response from various colleges across Pune.
